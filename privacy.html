Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Cocktail Matrix app ("the app") handles information. The app is provided by kodevmaker ("we", "us", "our").

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The app does not require an account and does not send analytics.

The app stores your "I made this cocktail" selections locally on your device so they persist between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

The app can open external links (for example, YouTube videos) in your browser or the YouTube app. These services have their own privacy policies and practices.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
